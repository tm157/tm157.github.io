Hide Google News

Long time ago, I was frustrated with Google News. I had customized it to filter out all entertainment news from my feed, but sadly Google thought it was better to do otherwise. To fix this I wrote a script that would allow me to remove certain news items from my feed.

The idea was to add a Hide News button below each news item which would dynamically remove the corresponding news item from the feed and would also index this news item, so that if one would ever refresh the page it would automatically remove the item from your view. I implemented the first part but never got around to do the latter one.
