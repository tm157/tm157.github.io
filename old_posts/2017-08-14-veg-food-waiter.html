Highlight Veg food only.

US is a predominant meat loving country. When I first moved here most of the food items on restaurant websites used to contain meat. To avoid wasting time while ordering something we wrote a simple script that would use Regex matching to find the veg food items in the webpage and hightlight them. This saves a ton of time.

Disclaimer: The original idea and implementation was by a colleague of mine (check github history).
